Our Story

Where Every Meal
Is a Memory

Thamaq Restaurant was founded with a simple dream: to bring the finest flavours of India, Arabia, and beyond to the heart of Bengaluru. Nestled on Bagalur Main Road, we have been serving our community for over 8 years.

Our chefs masterfully blend traditional recipes with contemporary techniques, ensuring every bite transports you to the origins of that cuisine — be it the North Indian frontier, the streets of Hyderabad, or the grills of Arabia.
